about this blog

Sun Jan 29, 2017 by me

I finally set up my blog after thinking about it for a while. It was easy and cheap thanks to the instructions on Superfluous Sextant. On the technical side, I decided to use Hugo instead of a Python static site generator1, even though I am a big fan of Python.

I started this blog in order to write about programming, data analysis, finance2, sports, interesting articles, books I am reading, and whatever else comes to mind.3 One of the models for this blog is Daring Fireball. I like how Gruber links to stories and offers plenty of snark and also writes longer opinion pieces.4
